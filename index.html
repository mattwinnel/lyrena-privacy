Lyrena Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2025

Lyrena is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store data when you use the Lyrena app.

Data We Collect

  • User Prompts: When you input a description for music generation, your prompt is sent to our servers for AI processing.
  • User ID: If you sign in with Apple, we collect your Apple User ID to manage your account and balance.
  • Usage Data: We collect basic information about how you use the app, such as the number of generations and token usage.
  • Performance Data: We collect data to monitor backend performance and ensure the app works reliably.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide core app functionality and generate music based on your prompts.
  • To manage your account balance and purchase history.
  • To monitor performance and fix errors or service issues.
  • To improve the app experience.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or tracking across apps.
  • We do not collect sensitive personal data like location, contacts, or financial details (purchases are processed securely by Apple).

Data Storage

Your prompts and associated files (music scores and audio) are temporarily stored on our servers to deliver the requested services. User balances and purchase information are also managed securely via our backend.
